Who We Are

Built on Trust,
Driven by Detail

Melbourne Chauffeur & Limo Service was founded in 2010 with a single car and a simple belief: that every passenger deserves to travel with dignity, comfort and certainty. What began as a one-man operation from Melbourne's inner suburbs has grown into one of Victoria's most respected private transport services.

We are a family-owned business — not a faceless rideshare platform. Every booking is handled by a real person, every driver is personally vetted, and every vehicle is maintained to the highest standard. When you travel with us, you are our only priority.

Our client base spans corporate executives, interstate visitors, wedding parties, sporting delegations and everyday Melburnians who simply refuse to compromise on quality. We treat every journey — whether a 10-minute airport run or a full-day corporate schedule — with the same level of care and professionalism.

We are fully CPV-accredited under VicRoads regulations, comprehensively insured, and all drivers undergo rigorous police checks and training before their first booking. Our fleet is refreshed regularly to ensure you always arrive in a late-model, immaculately presented vehicle.

What Drives Us

The Standards We Never Compromise

Six principles that define every journey we operate — from the moment you book to the moment you arrive.

Punctuality

Your time is not negotiable. We monitor traffic in real time and build buffer time into every journey so that late arrivals are not in our vocabulary.

Safety First

Every driver holds a current CPV accreditation, has passed comprehensive police checks, and maintains a clean driving record. Your safety is our primary obligation.

Genuine Hospitality

We open doors — literally and figuratively. Our drivers are courteous, discreet and attentive. We remember preferences and go out of our way to make you feel valued.

Transparent Pricing

The price we quote is the price you pay. No surge pricing, no hidden airport fees, no post-trip surprises. Fixed fares, always.

Absolute Discretion

What happens in our vehicles stays in our vehicles. We serve high-profile clients, executives and VIPs who depend on complete confidentiality — and we take that responsibility seriously.

Reliability

In 14 years of operation we have never left a client stranded. If something unexpected occurs, we have contingency protocols and backup vehicles to ensure you are never let down.

Our Journey

14 Years of Milestones

From a single vehicle to one of Victoria's most respected chauffeur services — here are the moments that shaped us.

2010
Founded in Melbourne

Launched with a single Mercedes-Benz E-Class and a commitment to premium service for corporate clients in the CBD.

2013
Airport Transfer Partnership

Established formal corporate contracts with Melbourne Airport meet-and-greet services, expanding fleet to five vehicles.

2016
Fleet Reaches Ten Vehicles

Introduced wedding and special events packages. Added Mercedes-Benz S-Class and the first stretch limousine to the fleet.

2020
COVID Response & Recovery

Pivoted to essential worker transfers, deep-clean protocols, and retained every member of our team throughout the pandemic.

2024
Hybrid & EV Fleet Expansion

Added Mercedes-Benz EQS and Tesla Model S to the fleet, reducing our carbon footprint without compromising on luxury.
